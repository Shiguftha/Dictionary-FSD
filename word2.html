Word: Bungalow

Definition & Usage

  • A low house with a broad front porch, having either no upper floor or upper rooms set in the roof.
  • Popular in tropical or subtropical regions.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
cottage, chalet, ranch apartment, mansion, duplex
