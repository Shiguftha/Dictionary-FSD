Word: Obfuscate

Definition & Usage

  • To make something unclear or obscure.
  • Usually done intentionally to confuse.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
confuse, obscure, blur clarify, reveal, explain

Obfuscate is often used in literature and descriptions to convey unique meanings. It may highlight deeper context or emotion in language.
