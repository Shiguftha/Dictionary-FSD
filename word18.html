Word: Resplendent

Definition & Usage

  • Attractive and impressive through being richly colorful or sumptuous.
  • Brilliant and splendid in appearance.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
brilliant, glorious, radiant dull, drab, plain

Resplendent is often used in literature and descriptions to convey unique meanings. It may highlight deeper context or emotion in language.
