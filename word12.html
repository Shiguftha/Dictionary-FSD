Word: Limerence

Definition & Usage

  • The state of being infatuated with another person.
  • Obsessive romantic desire.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
infatuation, crush, obsession disinterest, indifference, apathy

Limerence is often used in literature and descriptions to convey unique meanings. It may highlight deeper context or emotion in language.
