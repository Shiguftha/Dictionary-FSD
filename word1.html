Word: Abscond

Definition & Usage

  • To leave hurriedly and secretly, typically to avoid detection or arrest.
  • Used when someone escapes from a place they shouldn't leave.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
flee, escape, bolt stay, remain, face

Abscond is often used in literature and descriptions to convey unique meanings. It may highlight deeper context or emotion in language.
