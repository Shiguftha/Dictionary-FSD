Word: Ephemeral

Definition & Usage

  • Lasting for a very short time.
  • Often describes fleeting beauty or short-lived events.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
transient, brief, momentary enduring, lasting, permanent

Ephemeral is often used in literature and descriptions to convey unique meanings. It may highlight deeper context or emotion in language.
