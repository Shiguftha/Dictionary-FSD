Word: Cacophony

Definition & Usage

  • A harsh discordant mixture of sounds.
  • Often used to describe noisy environments.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
din, racket, discord harmony, melody, silence

Cacophony is often used in literature and descriptions to convey unique meanings. It may highlight deeper context or emotion in language.
