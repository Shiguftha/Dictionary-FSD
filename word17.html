Word: Quixotic

Definition & Usage

  • Exceedingly idealistic; unrealistic and impractical.
  • Based on or resembling Don Quixote.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
idealistic, romantic, dreamy practical, realistic, sensible

Quixotic is often used in literature and descriptions to convey unique meanings. It may highlight deeper context or emotion in language.
