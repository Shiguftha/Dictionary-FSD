Word: Juxtapose

Definition & Usage

  • To place side by side, especially for comparison or contrast.
  • Often used in art and design.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
compare, collocate, align separate, isolate, detach

Juxtapose is often used in literature and descriptions to convey unique meanings. It may highlight deeper context or emotion in language.
