Word: Petrichor

Definition & Usage

  • The pleasant smell that frequently accompanies the first rain after a long period of warm, dry weather.
  • Caused by oils released from the ground.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
earthy smell, rain scent odorless, stench

Petrichor is often used in literature and descriptions to convey unique meanings. It may highlight deeper context or emotion in language.
