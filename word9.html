Word: Ineffable

Definition & Usage

  • Too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words.
  • Indescribable, beyond words.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
indescribable, unutterable, transcendent describable, expressible, definable

Ineffable is often used in literature and descriptions to convey unique meanings. It may highlight deeper context or emotion in language.
