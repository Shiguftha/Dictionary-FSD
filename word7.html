Word: Gargantuan

Definition & Usage

  • Enormous, gigantic in size or amount.
  • Often used to exaggerate the size of objects.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
huge, colossal, massive tiny, minute, small

Gargantuan is often used in literature and descriptions to convey unique meanings. It may highlight deeper context or emotion in language.
