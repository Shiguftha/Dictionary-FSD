Word: Doppelgänger

Definition & Usage

  • A non-biologically related look-alike or double of a living person.
  • Sometimes used to describe ghostly counterparts.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
double, twin, clone original, opposite, unique

Doppelgänger is often used in literature and descriptions to convey unique meanings. It may highlight deeper context or emotion in language.
